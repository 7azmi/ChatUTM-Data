IMPLEMENTATION OF TEACHING AND LEARNING (T&L) ONLINE ON 29 OCTOBER 2024 UNTIL 30 OCTOBER 2024 IN CELEBRATION OF DEEPAVALI


Referring to Academic Circular No. 2/2024 dated 2 April 2024, which outlines the UTM Academic Calendar Session 2024/2025 for both Undergraduate and Postgraduate students, along with Guidelines On Exemption From Lectures During Public/Festive Holidays, as well as Online Learning Procedures (Academic Circular No. 3/2023 dated 7 August 2023), the public holiday for the Deepavali celebration on 31 October 2024 (Thursday) falls within the lecture session week.
