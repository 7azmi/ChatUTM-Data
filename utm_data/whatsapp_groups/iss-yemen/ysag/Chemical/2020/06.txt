Listed below are the SOP's for online Final Examination which are applicable for subjects offered by SCEE only. 

There are 3 steps for the online final exam process. 

Step 1: Before exam (Fill in forms, eg. Form 1, Form 2, Form 3)
Step 2: During exam (For exam longer than 90 minutes, exam will be divided into two sessions (eg. Part 1, Submit, Break, Part 2, Submit)
Step 3: After exam

Example - Three (3) hours final exam equivalent to 6 questions

•       Scan Attendance + Download Question Part 1 :  15 mins
•       Answer Part 1  : 90 mins (3 Questions)
•       Submit Interim Part 1 :  10 mins (optional)
•       Submit Final Answer Part 1 :  15 mins
•       Break  :  30 mins
•       Download Question Part 2 :  10 mins
•       Answer Part 2 : 90 mins (3 Questions)
•       Submit Interim Part 2 :  10 mins (optional)
•       Submit Final Answer Part 2  :  15 mins
•       Submission Buffer Time :  15 mins

             Total : 300 mins (5 hours)

Morning session

After (Online): 8.30am - 1.30pm

Afternoon session
