[CLARIFICATION ON SKE TnL METHOD]

‎السَّلاَمُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَرَحْمَةُ اللهِ وَبَرَكَاتُهُ, 你好, வணக்கம்,
Greetings to all UTMians

Following enquiries from several parties, especially from students of School of Electrical Engineering (SKE) on Teaching and Learning (TnL) methods, we are also aware that students are only given one month for preparation to enter campus. It is our responsibility to obtain information and explanation from the Associate Chair (Academic and Student Development) of the School of Electrical Engineering, Associate Professor Dr Affendi regarding this matter. 

The following is the chronology of the issuance of notice(s) regarding the teaching and learning methods:

1. The first notice that was issued by SKE dated 14 March 2022 stated that face-to-face laboratory implementations will begin on the Week 5 (17 April 2022). Next, the implementation of TnL is online until the end of semester 2 2021/2022 while the final examinations will be conducted physically (10 to 28 July 2022).

2. Issuance of Academic Circular No. 2/2022 dated 31 March 2022 states all TnL activities are carried out physically and face-to-face or in a blended manner.

3. The second notice dated April 6, 2022 states the change of the TnL method where it will be conducted face-to-face/physically starting Week 7 (May 8, 2022).

Both of these notices have been issued by SKE, taking into account the considerations of various parties to ensure the most effective teaching and learning methods for students. (Please refer to the link for further information)

Students who are constrained from entering campus (eg: health, financial, family etc) after the mid-semester break may inform their lecturers and academic advisors on their current whereabouts to determine the learning method. (Subjected to the discretion of the lecturer and approval of the Faculty Academic Committee). However, students will need to ensure that they are on campus before the final exam which will be conducted physically. 

Further discussions on this issue will be held with the School Chair, Prof. Ir. Dr. Mohd Wazir nearest future. We take note of all suggestions and problems raised by SKE students. We humbly seek your understanding and patience regarding this matter.

SKE: Student Representative Council

_يمكنكم التواصل مع رئيس الاتحاد عمر فهمي بشكل خاص لاخذ استشارة فيما اذا كانت دوافعكم وصعوباتكم سيتم اخذها بعين الاعتبار ام لا وتقديم اي نصائح قد تساعدكم في طلبكم للكلية_
